    About Us

    Pique is a one-page scrolling theme designed for small, independent businesses. Using the Customizer settings, you can tweak and adjust each panel until it looks just right.

    Support for site logos, social media links, and testimonials is baked in, and you also have access to a comprehensive library of icons to add some interest to your pages. It’s easy to add your contact information, hours of operation, and a contact form so that your customers can easily find you. And of course, it’s all accessible on a phone or tablet, as well a computer, so your customers will be able to find your information even when they’re on the go.

    Ready to put a little pep in your business? Take Pique for a spin!
